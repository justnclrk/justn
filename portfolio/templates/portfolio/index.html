about

The product of two very hard working parents and the youngest of five boys. I was always allowed to be myself which led to me being able to develop a strong imagination and the desire to connect people through new and different experiences.

I began coding at the end of 2017 and immediately fell in love with the idea of being able to create and build whatever I could think of or solve an existing problem with a few lines of code.
